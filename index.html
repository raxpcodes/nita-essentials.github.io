What is NITA Essentials?

A guide built from real experience, not guesswork

When I joined NIT Agartala, I spent weeks asking seniors, browsing forums, and figuring out what to buy, what to study, and what to avoid. Most of that information was scattered. NITA Essentials brings it all together - vetted recommendations from students who have actually been through it.
