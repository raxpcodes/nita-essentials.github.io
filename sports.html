Basketball · Apparel

Sports Jersey

Essential. Any sports jersey is fine the key thing is that it doesn't absorb and hold sweat. Look for polyester/mesh fabric that releases moisture quickly. Any brand works.

Any breathable jersey works
Basketball · Accessories

Sports Socks (Pack)

Any sports socks work, just make sure you have at least 3 pairs. Sounds trivial, but you'll thank yourself later. Having enough pairs means you're never stuck with wet socks before a game.
